About Autranz

This project was made initially for the purpose of helping people with hearing impairment, this is our way of making life easier for these categories of people, we decided to also expand this application to other users who don't feel like going through the stress of typing. We were also inspired to include the translation section ,so that users can easily transcribe and translate languages they do not understand, We will look to implement these features as time goes on. This project is dedicated to ALX and Holberton school.
